Overview
We're looking for a Digital Marketing Apprentice to join our team and complete the Multi-Channel Marketer Apprenticeship with NowSkills. You'll work closely with our digital team, supporting blog writing, case study creation, landing page writing, social media support across Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn and more.
Description
  • Help write engaging ad copy, social media captions, and marketing content
  • Support content creation, including blogs, case studies, and website copy
  • Create images and short videos using tools such as Canva
  • Support organic social media management
  • Learn how to research keywords, audiences, and trends
  • Support landing page ideas and basic testing to improve results
  • Ensure brand tone and messaging are consistent across different clients
  • Assist with paid advertising campaigns across platforms such as Google Ads, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), LinkedIn and TikTok
