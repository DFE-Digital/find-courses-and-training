Meet Katy

Admin assistant Katy has worked in administration for over 10 years, supporting people in a range of sectors.

My name is Katy. I am a services support assistant for Breast Cancer Now. I've done a bit of office management as well, including administrative duties for theatre.

My current role involves being that first point of contact for any person diagnosed with breast cancer who wants to access our services.

My favourite thing about my job is helping people do what they want to be able to do. Being able to support people to expand the service we provide is really rewarding for me.
