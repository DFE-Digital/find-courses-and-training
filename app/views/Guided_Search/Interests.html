Add search terms

Search for courses and training by telling us up to 3 key search terms.

You can search by things like school subjects, practical skills, hobbies and interests to find courses and training that could be right for you.

Be as specific or as broad as you like. The terms do not have to link to each other, they can all be totally different things if you’d prefer.

The search terms search will use AI, helping to bring back results most relevant to you.

For example, you could enter:

  • maths
  • bricklaying
  • working with animals
