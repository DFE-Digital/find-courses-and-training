Overview
This T Level is for students with an interest in technology and digital innovation. If you have an interest in coding, digital design, and development, and you aspire to work in the tech industry, this course is ideal for you. The T Level is a 2-year programme and will include a 9 week (minimum) industry placement as well as a Technical Qualification.
