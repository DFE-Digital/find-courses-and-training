Find courses and training

Search for courses and training, including apprenticeships, and get support with your choices.

Choose how you'd like to find courses and training. Both the quick search and guided search options will show you a list of results for opportunities to suit your needs.

Quick search

Enter search terms for a faster way to get your results and filter them. This option is good if you have an idea of what you'd like to do.

Guided search

Enter search terms and make choices step by step, letting us know what will work best for you. At the end, you'll see a list of pre-filtered results.

Take a skills assessment

If you're not sure where to start, answer questions about your interests to find out which sectors may be best for you.

Explore careers

Choose a way to explore careers. For each job profile, you'll find qualifications needed, salary, progression opportunities and a description of daily tasks involved.

Get course or training support

See what support you can get with funding, childcare or disability adjustments. Or, get help to choose the course or training that best suits you.

Understand course and training types

Visit Skills for Careers to find out more about the different course types and training options.
